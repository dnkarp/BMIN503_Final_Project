Services We Provide

TNEP currently provides the following services in Pierce County: access to sterile syringes and other injecting equipment; safe collection and proper disposal of used syringes; condom distribution; distribution of information regarding safer injecting, safer sexual practices and risk reduction; referral to social, health and welfare services; opiate overdose prevention and naloxone distribution; and much more!
